64  :func:`model.utils.bbox_tools.bbox2loc`.

66 
67  Here are notations.
68 
69  * :math:`S` is the total number of sampled RoIs, which equals \

71  * :math:`L` is number of object classes possibly including the \
72  background.
73 
74  Args:
75  roi (array): Region of Interests (RoIs) from which we sample.
76  Its shape is :math:`(R, 4)`
77  bbox (array): The coordinates of ground truth bounding boxes.
78  Its shape is :math:`(R', 4)`.
79  label (array): Ground truth bounding box labels. Its shape
80  is :math:`(R',)`. Its range is :math:`[0, L - 1]`, where
81  :math:`L` is the number of foreground classes.
82  loc_normalize_mean (tuple of four floats): Mean values to normalize
83  coordinates of bouding boxes.
84  loc_normalize_std (tupler of four floats): Standard deviation of
85  the coordinates of bounding boxes.
86 
87  Returns:
88  (array, array, array):
